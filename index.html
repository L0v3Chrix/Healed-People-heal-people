Chapter 01The idea

The thesis

Redirect local advertising value into a human exchange.

Every day, businesses spend money shouting into feeds. StickerPass takes that same value and hands it to a neighbor — as a small piece of collectible art with a second life.

✋

Community distributor

Begins as the giver, passes collectible art, and sees transparent verified activity and approved value.

The giver
✨

Recipient

Receives a collectible, understands the exchange, and discovers an optional relevant local offer.

The neighbor
🏪

Local business

Receives an authenticated visit, a controlled offer workflow, and aggregate measurable participation.

Chapter 02How it moves

The complete beta proof

Physical to digital to accountable.

Eight steps. One dashed line from a printed sticker to a verified, human-approved obligation. Nothing skips a step.

01

Mint

Generate a unique controlled code.

02

Print

Proof, scan-test, activate, and pack.

03

Pass

A distributor gives the sticker first.

04

Reveal

A public scan explains and unlocks.

05

Redeem

Authenticated staff verify the offer.

06

Account

One verified event creates one obligation.

07

Review

Humans approve and record execution.

08

Report

Inventory and aggregate impact reconcile.

The rule that protects the model A scan alone never moves money. Only a verified redemption may create an approved participant-value obligation.

Chapter 06The fuel

Recommended financing path

Let the community invest in the company behind the mission.

Recommended

Wefunder Community Round

A Regulation Crowdfunding offering using a SAFE, subject to entity, counsel, CPA, platform, financial, and disclosure approval.

  • Potential future equity — not guaranteed ROI.
  • No scheduled debt service during the pre-revenue beta.
  • Up to five costed, mission-aligned investor perks.
  • Six-week audience warmup before launch.
Internal model$100KMinimum proof scenario
Internal model$250KRecommended target scenario
Internal model$500KMaximum / scale-reserve scenario

Scenario amounts are planning models only. Public terms require a bottom-up sourced budget, signed platform documents, counsel/CPA review, and approved disclosures. Investors may lose all invested capital.

Chapter 07The path

Critical path

Build trust before scale.

  1. G0
    Complete

    Evidence & planning

    Three-pass review, interview, PDR, scope, architecture, campaign and implementation plans.

  2. G1
    Next

    Control & entity

    Git baseline, owners, advisers, issuer/cap table, partner/business outreach, claims and risk control.

  3. G2
    Next

    Physical & workflow proof

    Tested sample sticker and a transparent staged end-to-end demonstration.

  4. G3
    Gate

    Campaign readiness

    SAFE, Form C inputs, financials, budget, page, video, risks, perks, warm audience.

  5. G4
    Gate

    Community Round

    Platform-approved public raise and investor communications.

  6. G5
    Fund-dependent

    Beta build

    Inventory, recipient, business, partner, finance, operator, privacy and support workflows.

  7. G6
    Release gate

    Pilot rehearsal

    Partner, finance, privacy, insurance, print, accessibility, security, restore and incident checks.

  8. G7
    Real proof

    Controlled Austin beta

    Start small, review daily, expand only after evidence.

  9. G8
    Decision

    Continue, revise, pause, or stop

    Evidence report, remediation backlog, investor update, and accountable public report.

Christmas 2026 is an objective, not a promise. The responsible fallback is pilot-ready or controlled-beta status by Christmas with the exit decision in January 2027.
